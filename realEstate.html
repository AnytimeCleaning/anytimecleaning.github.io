Detail Home Cleanings

A basic home cleaning includes a top to bottom left to right cleaning of your home. Bathrooms get a thorough scrubbing including, light fixtures, fans, mirrors, vanity, toilets and shower/tub. Main living gets a detailed dusting, edge vacuum, furniture vacuum and floor cleaning. Kitchens include countertops, sinks, stove tops, cabinet fronts, inside of microwaves and floors. Bedrooms are similar to main living cleaning adding in sheet changes/bed making. Mudrooms get a basic dusting and thorough floor scrubbing. Throughout the house garbage and recycling is taken out, blinds are dry dusted or vacuumed, baseboards dusted and stairs are vacuumed.

I sulppy gental cleaners, rags, vacuumes and cleaning tools.

Extra details can be added. Polish woodwork, clean screens, trim and windows. Sweep out garage. Clean inside of appliances. Vacuum and dust closets.
